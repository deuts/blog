GCash faces another bank transfer glitch

Just days after users reported unauthorized deductions from their accounts, popular e-wallet platform GCash faced another issue. On Friday afternoon, the Ayala-backed company announced that its bank transfer feature via BancNet was experiencing problems, though the cause was unclear.

I guess I joined the unlucky club, as I was a victim of this GCash bank transfer glitch yesterday. The silver lining? My funds were credited to my GCash account today, after a 24-hour wait. Talk about an extended transfer “experience”!
