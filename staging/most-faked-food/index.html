11 Most Faked Foods In The World

September 5, 2023 | Edit

Insider Business shows 11 of the most faked foods in the world, which include:

  • Truffles
  • Maple Syrup
  • Wasabi
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Vanilla
  • Caviar
  • Honey
  • Olive Oil
  • Wagyu Beef
  • Coffee
  • Saffron
 

Of the 11, I would say fake parmesan cheese, honey and olive oil are the most I probably am guilty of consuming. I realize this now, I should be cautious when I’m doing my groceries next time.
